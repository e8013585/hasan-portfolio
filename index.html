About Me

35+ Years of Excellence

Creative Director & Graphic Designer

Hello, I'm Hasan — a Creative Director, Art Director, and Graphic Designer with 30+ years of experience in international magazine publishing, editorial design, and brand development. I've worked with leading global media groups including Condé Nast Magazine Group (New York–based), Hearst Communications, Doğuş Media Group, and Haymarket Media Group, contributing to renowned titles such as Vogue, GQ, National Geographic, Esquire, Robb Report, Billboard, EVO, CNBC-e Business, NG Kids, Autocar, Stuff, T3, PlayStation, FHM, and Yachting World.

A pioneer of digital magazine publishing across iOS and Android platforms, I specialize in modern magazine design, Adobe Creative tools, and crafting compelling visual narratives with a strong eye for detail. I'm currently serving as Creative Director of Envoy Magazine, distributed throughout the U.S., Canada, Europe, and Turkey, creating design work that resonates with audiences worldwide.

About Envoy

Envoy Magazine is an internationally distributed lifestyle and culture publication that I currently serve as Creative Director for. The magazine covers a diverse range of topics including travel, culture, cuisine, art, fashion, and global perspectives — crafted for a discerning readership that values quality editorial content and sophisticated visual design.

Since its inception, I have been responsible for the complete visual identity of Envoy — from logo design and brand guidelines to every single page layout, cover design, typography selection, and photo direction. Each issue is meticulously crafted to deliver a cohesive, immersive reading experience that balances editorial depth with visual elegance.

Envoy is actively printed and sold in major bookstores nationwide and internationally, including retailers across the United States, various regions in Europe, Canada, and Türkiye. The magazine bridges Eastern and Western cultures, offering readers unique stories and perspectives from around the world.
