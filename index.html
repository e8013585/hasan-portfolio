About me

Creative Director & Graphic Designer

I'm Hasan — a California-based Creative Director, Art Director, and Graphic Designer with over three decades of experience in international magazine publishing, editorial design, and brand development. I've worked with leading global media groups including Condé Nast (New York), Hearst Communications, Doğuş Media Group, and Haymarket Media Group, contributing to titles such as Vogue, GQ, National Geographic, Esquire, Robb Report, Billboard, EVO, Autocar, Stuff, T3, PlayStation, FHM, and Yachting World.

A pioneer of digital magazine publishing across iOS and Android platforms, I specialise in modern magazine design, the Adobe Creative suite, and crafting compelling visual narratives with a strong eye for detail. I currently serve as Creative Director of Envoy Magazine — distributed across the US, Canada, Europe, and Turkey.

About Envoy

Envoy Magazine is an internationally distributed lifestyle and culture publication that I currently serve as Creative Director for. Each issue covers travel, culture, cuisine, art, fashion, and global perspectives — crafted for a readership that values quality editorial content and sophisticated visual design.

Since its inception, I have been responsible for the complete visual identity of Envoy — from the logo and brand guidelines through to every page layout, cover design, typography selection, and photo direction. Each issue is meticulously produced to deliver a cohesive, immersive reading experience that balances editorial depth with visual elegance.

Envoy is printed and sold in major bookstores across the United States, Europe, Canada, and Türkiye. The magazine bridges Eastern and Western cultures, offering readers unique stories and perspectives from around the world.
